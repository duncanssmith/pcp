The comprehensive media database for Asia.

Connecting PRs & The Media Across Asia

Telum Media provides a pan Asia media database for PR professionals. We are run by former journalism & PR professionals who saw the need for a comprehensive media database to save time for both PRs and journalists.

For PRs

Telum Media provides a media database for PR professionals covering the complete media & journalist scene in Singapore, Malaysia, Indonesia, Hong Kong & Australia.

We also cover the key media for all sectors across the rest of Asia incorporating China, Cambodia, Myanmar, Philippines, Taiwan, Thailand and Vietnam.

Our data is constantly expanding, guided by the needs of our clients, and our user friendly system saves your team time by allowing quick searching for journalists and / or publications by country, region, sector, job title and much more. Users can also use our Media Knowledge Management System to manage their media interactions, build and maintain contact lists and export and distribute them.

Telum Media's system enables your team to target the right media outlet(s) / journalist(s) using up to date and accurate information ensuring good communication between both communities.

For journalists

Whether you have a job change to announce, sector(s) update, new publication launch, a vacancy in your team that you would like to advertise or any other editorial news, Telum Media Alerts ensure that you can update the media to receive only relevant content, stories and job applicants. All alerts are signed off by the journalist(s) concerned.
